ACSS UPDATE

 ACSS pride Jio Chua and team hailed champions of Huawei ICT Competition 2021 - CLOUD track

The now 4th year BS Computer Science student and former ACSS Vice-President Internal Jio Vince Chua, along with other EN students Jerwin Samala and Mark Jayson Guiterrez, aced the "CLOUD track" division of the recently held Huawei ICT Competition 2021.

Carrying the name of UE, they now advance and represent the Philippines in the Asia Pacific Round with students from Mapua University who finished first-runner up. The said competition was contended by only the best and brightest ICT talents throughout the Philippines.

Your ACSS fam would like to praise our ACSS pride and congratulate the whole team for their achievements! We also wish you the best of luck and we look forward as the team represents the Philippines in the upcoming Asia Pacific Round.
