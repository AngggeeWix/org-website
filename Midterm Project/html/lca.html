Lights, Camera, ACSSion!

 If you consider yourself a photophile or a shutterbug, then this event is perfect for you! Just check the following guidelines for further information.

  1. This event is open to all College of Engineering students.
  2. Only ONE (1) entry per participant is allowed.
  3. The subject theme of the photograph should be related to the "College of Engineering" or "University of the East."
  4. There should be a 2-3 sentence-long description of the subject upon submission.
  5. Only minimal editing of your photo is allowed.
  6. Due to restrictions, we highly encourage you to stay at home during the course of the event to avoid any problems.
